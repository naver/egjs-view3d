Center of the camera distance calculation.
If `"auto"` is given, it will use the center of the model's bounding box.
Else, you can use any world position.
The difference to [pivot](/docs/options/control/pivot) is model's bounding box and center position will be shown on screen at every rotation angle.

## Example
### center: "auto" (default)
<View3D
  src="/egjs-view3d/model/draco/alarm.glb"
  center="auto"
  showExampleCode />

### center: [0.03, 0, 0]
<View3D
  src="/egjs-view3d/model/draco/alarm.glb"
  center={[0.03, 0, 0]}
  showExampleCode />

### center: ["0%", "50%", "100%"]
<View3D
  src="/egjs-view3d/model/draco/alarm.glb"

  showExampleCode />
